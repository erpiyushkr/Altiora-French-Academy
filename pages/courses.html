FAQ

Answers about our French courses

Everything you need to know before joining.

What is TEF Canada? +

TEF Canada is a French language exam accepted for Canadian immigration and CRS scoring.

What is TCF Canada? +

TCF Canada is another approved French exam for immigration, similar to TEF.

How long to reach CLB 7? +

With consistent live classes, LMS work, and weekly assessments, most learners reach CLB 7 in about 9–10 months.

Do you provide LMS? +

Yes. All tracks include LMS access with assignments, quizzes, and replays.

Do you offer speaking practice? +

Yes. Weekly speaking sessions are included in every program.

Are mock tests included? +

Weekly assessments and mock tests are built into the schedule.

What is batch size? +

Small group classes are capped at six students for personalized attention.

Do you provide personal coaching? +

Yes. One-to-one coaching with customized pricing and roadmap is available.

What is course duration? +

TEF/TCF tracks run 9–10 months; level-based personal coaching ranges from 3 to 6 months by level.

Do you provide certification? +

We provide course completion proof and readiness reports; official certificates are issued by the TEF/TCF exam bodies.

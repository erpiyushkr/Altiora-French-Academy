Why learn French for Canada immigration? +

French boosts your CRS score and eligibility for provinces like Quebec. We align prep to immigration needs.

Which exam should I take for Canada immigration? +

Both TEF and TCF are accepted. We’ll assess your goals and timeline to recommend the best fit.

What level is required for TEF Canada? +

Most applicants target B2 or higher for competitive CRS points. We roadmap you to that level with milestones.

How long does it take to reach B2 level? +

Most learners reach B2 in 8–12 months with consistent study and live practice. Intensive paths are available.

Do you offer personalized coaching? +

Yes. One-to-one coaching plans are tailored to your timeline, strengths, and target scores.

Are classes live or recorded? +

Live instructor-led classes with replays provided so you can revise on your own schedule.

Do you provide exam mock tests? +

Yes. Timed TEF/TCF mocks with scoring rubrics and instructor feedback are included.
